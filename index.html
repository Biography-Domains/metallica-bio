Metallica

Thrash Metal, Heavy Metal, Hard Rock

Metallica, formed in 1981 in Los Angeles, California, is an American heavy metal band that has become one of the most influential and commercially successful bands in music history. The band's origins trace back to when drummer Lars Ulrich placed an advertisement in a Los Angeles newspaper looking for other musicians interested in forming a band.
